About the blog

Hi, my name is Aga. I used to work in a HR department, until I decided I needed a complete life-reboot and jumped at the first idea I had: to learn programming.

As a total newbie, I started with online courses, articles, tutorials, workshops and meetings with local IT groups. Practice makes perfect, so I used my newly learned skills to create this blog. I decided to dedicate it to documenting my learning journey and share with you my views on the learning resources available. So far, my areas of interest were Python, Django, SQL, HTML and CSS.

If you'd like to share your learning experiences or suggest a course, or a book, I should check, leave a comment or contact me.
